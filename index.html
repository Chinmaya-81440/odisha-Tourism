When to Visit

Oct–Mar offers pleasant weather for temples, wildlife and beaches. Monsoons (Jul–Sep) turn forests lush and lakes dramatic.

Getting Around

Travel Basics

Fly into Bhubaneswar (BBI). Puri & Konark are 1–2 hours by road. Book eco-stays near Chilika; reserve forest lodges for Similipal in advance.
