About Odisha Tourism

India’s Best Kept Secret—told through heritage, coasts and wild sanctuaries. Here’s our story and mission.

Our Mission

Inspire responsible travel across Odisha by celebrating culture, conserving nature, and empowering local communities.

  • Showcase authentic experiences—temple trails, beaches, wetlands, forests and crafts.
  • Support eco-stays and community guides for inclusive growth.
  • Preserve heritage and biodiversity for future generations.
