FAQs

What’s the best time to visit?

October to March for pleasant weather. Monsoons (Jul–Sep) are lush—great for lakes & forests, but check road conditions.

How many days are ideal?

3–4 days for a temple+coast snapshot; 5–6 if you’re adding Chilika or Similipal; 7+ to slow-travel with crafts & food trails.

Are permits needed for Similipal?

Yes, often. Book forest lodges & permits in advance; entry may be closed on some days—confirm locally before travel.
