Top Destinations of Odisha

Discover the most captivating places that showcase Odisha’s heritage, nature, and culture.

Explore the Gems of Odisha

Beach & Culture

Puri – The Holy City

Home to Lord Jagannath Temple and golden beaches, Puri is one of India’s Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

UNESCO Heritage

Konark – The Sun Temple

The architectural marvel shaped like a colossal chariot, symbolizing the Sun God’s journey across the heavens.

Lake & Wildlife

Chilika Lake

Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon, home to Irrawaddy dolphins and thousands of migratory birds.

Temple City

Bhubaneswar

Known as the “City of Temples,” it blends ancient heritage with a modern urban charm.

National Park

Similipal

A biodiversity hotspot featuring tigers, elephants, and beautiful waterfalls like Barehipani and Joranda.

Peace Pagoda

Dhauli

The site of the historic Kalinga War, now a symbol of peace with the beautiful Shanti Stupa on the banks of River Daya.
